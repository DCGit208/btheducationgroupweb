Investment & Sponsorship Opportunities

Our programmes succeed thanks to mission‑driven partners who invest in equipment or sponsor students. Investors provide critical assets (e.g., tractors, drilling rigs, solar panels, Starlink terminals) and earn residual income by leasing them to training centres. Sponsors fund course fees for groups of learners (10, 50 or 100 participants), ensuring that no one is left behind.

  • Investor packages: fund sector‑specific equipment and receive returns from rentals and service concessions.
  • Sponsorship tiers: Bronze (10 learners), Silver (50 learners), Gold (100 learners) — approximately $170 per learner (100 000 FCFA).
  • Multi‑sector reach: direct your investment or sponsorship to any of our programmes: agriculture, energy, WASH, construction, health, digital skills, business, crafts, environment, transport, safety, communications, research, education, civic tech, biotech, automotive, web development, agritech, connectivity or aerospace.

Contact us to discuss partnership options or to sponsor a cohort of students in the sector that resonates most with you.
