### 1.3 Role definitions

**SWI Partner — Strategic Workforce Infrastructure Partner**  
Executive-level institutional participant responsible for governance, ecosystem stewardship, divisional oversight, workforce infrastructure expansion, and strategic coordination.

**SWD-INV — Strategic Workforce Development Infrastructure Participant**  
Participant responsible for workforce expansion, portfolio development, institutional coordination, deployment continuity, and workforce infrastructure scaling. **NOT an investor.**

**WDS — Workforce Development Structure**  
Operational structure responsible for workforce production, deployment readiness, continuity, cohort operations, and performance supervision.

**WDDAM — Develop · Deploy · Acquire · Govern**  
Workforce lifecycle architecture governing production, deployment, acquisition, and governance of deployable professional capacity. **Not** training, recruitment, or staffing.

### 1.4 Data honesty rule (binding)

The economy must feel **operational** — but every displayed metric must be one of:

| State | Label in UI | Meaning |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **ARCHITECTURE** | `ARCHITECTURE` or unlabeled (fixed portfolio math) | Approved structural capacity from Portfolio Economics Appendix |
| **OPERATIONAL** | `OPERATIONAL` + timestamp + source | Verified activity from a defined data source or API |
| **MODELED** | `MODELED` or `FRAMEWORK VIEW` | Scenario, projection, or illustrative indicator — never presented as verified live market data |

**Never** label a metric `LIVE` or `ACTIVE` unless it is backed by a defined operational data source.  
**Never** simulate a live financial market without institutional provenance.  
**Never** invent portfolio figures not listed in Section 3.

Indices are **operational performance indices**, not tradable securities. Do not use crypto/token aesthetics.
