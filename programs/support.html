Popular FAQs

How do I enroll in a certification course?
Open the Certification Catalog, pick a course, and click Start Course. You’ll be guided to the Learning Hub to complete enrollment.
How are payments handled?
Payments are securely processed via Stripe. You’ll receive a receipt by email. For invoice or purchase orders, contact support below.
Where do I schedule my certification exam?
Exams are delivered through Pearson VUE and vendor partners. You’ll get scheduling instructions after course enrollment.
Can I reschedule or cancel?
Yes—policies vary by vendor. Contact us with your order details and target exam date, and we’ll help.
Do you offer group/corporate packages?
Yes. We can tailor programs for teams and enterprise with reporting and manager dashboards.
