

# Microsoft Certifications by Level

_Source: internal outline “Microsoft Market areas and Certification Levels”._

This document organizes key Microsoft certification pathways by **level** and **market area**. Each level builds on the previous one, layering additional certifications to move from fundamentals to expert and specialty roles.
