Investment & Sponsorship Opportunities

Investor Programme

Help us scale these vocational programmes by funding the tools, equipment and infrastructure needed for each sector. Rather than donating outright, investors purchase or lease assets – tractors, solar panels, water‑purification units and other critical gear – and earn residual revenue from their long‑term use in our training centres.

Examples of investment needs:

  • Agriculture: small tractors, irrigation pumps, seeders and threshers
  • Water & Sanitation: borehole drilling rigs, water filters and composting latrine kits
  • Renewable Energy: solar panels, batteries, inverters and mini‑grid controllers
  • Construction: block‑making machines, scaffolding and power tools
  • Healthcare: basic medical kits, diagnostic devices and training mannequins
  • Digital & Connectivity: laptops, routers and networking gear
  • Manufacturing & Crafts: welding sets, sewing machines and woodworking tools
  • Transportation: toolkits for bicycle/motorcycle repair and small engines
  • Public Safety: first‑aid kits, fire extinguishers and training props
  • ISP & Connectivity: Starlink terminals, antennas and power systems

Returns are generated through rental fees paid by training centres and cooperatives; investors retain ownership while communities build capacity. Contact us to discuss a tailored investment package for your preferred sector.
