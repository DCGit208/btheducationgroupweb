Our Story

15+ Years of Education Investment Excellence

Founded in 2010, BTH Education Group emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between education and economic opportunity through strategic investment in human capital development.

What began as a focused initiative to strengthen educational institutions has evolved into the world's leading education investment organization, serving over 50,000 professionals across 120+ countries.

Our unique dual approach combines Education Investment (developing institutions and workforce capacity) with Investment Education (teaching financial literacy and investment strategies) to create comprehensive pathways for individual and institutional growth.

Today, we partner with Fortune 500 companies, government agencies, and international organizations to deliver world-class professional development programs that drive measurable career transformation and economic impact.
