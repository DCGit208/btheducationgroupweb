## CERTIFICATION LEVELS & MAPPING

### Level 1 - Foundation (Entry Level)
- **G1**: Auto Maintenance & Light Repair
- **A Series Level 1**: A1, A5, A6, A7 (basic systems)
- **P2**: Automobile Parts Specialist (entry level)
- **C1**: Service Consultant

### Level 2 - Associate (Core Competency)
- **A Series Full**: A1-A8 (Master Auto Technician path)
- **B Series**: B2-B5 (collision repair)
- **P1, P4**: Specialized parts roles

### Level 3 - Professional (Advanced)
- **T Series**: T1-T8 (commercial truck technician)
- **S Series**: S1-S7 (school bus technician)
- **H Series**: H1-H8 (transit bus technician)
- **E Series**: E1-E3 (equipment installer)
- **L1, L2**: Advanced specialists

### Level 4 - Expert (Master Specialist)
- **L3**: Hybrid/Electric Vehicle Specialist
- **L4**: ADAS Specialist
- **MIL Series**: Military vehicle technician
- **P3**: Advanced Performance Parts

### Level 5 - Master (Multi-Discipline)
- **Master Certifications**: Holding multiple master statuses
- **Training & Leadership**: Instructor certifications
- **Multi-Series Mastery**: e.g., Master Auto + Master Truck

## RECERTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS

- **Validity**: 5 years from test date
- **Recertification Options**:
  - Retake full test
  - Take recertification test (shorter)
  - A-Series Renewal Program (online)
- **Master Status**: All component tests must remain current

---

## WORK EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS

- **2 years** hands-on experience in the relevant area
- Or **1 year** experience + relevant training program completion
- Military experience may substitute
- ASE Education Foundation programs provide substitution credit

---

## CAREER PATHWAYS

### Entry Path (0-2 years):
G1 → A1, A5, A7 → A6, A8 → Full A Series Master

### Collision Path:
G1 → B2 or B3 → B4 → B5 → B6 (estimator)

### Heavy Duty Path:
A Series → T2, T4, T6 → Full T Series Master

### Advanced Technology Path:
A8 → L1 → L3 → L4 (ADAS)

### Parts Professional Path:
P2 → P4 or P1 → P3 (performance)

### Service Advisor Path:
G1 → A Series knowledge → C1
