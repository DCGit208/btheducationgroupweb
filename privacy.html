Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

BTH Education Group (“BTH”, “we”) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information submitted through our institutional workforce engagement channels, including workforce-engage.html.

Information we collect

When you submit an executive briefing, cohort, or proposal request, we may collect: organisation name, contact details, role, country, sector interest, programme context, and optional NDA preference.

How we use information

  • To route your enquiry to the appropriate Sector Workforce Director or institutional lead
  • To respond within our stated service level (typically two business days for qualified institutional requests)
  • To prepare proposals, NDAs, and diligence materials where applicable

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared with BTH affiliates and delivery partners only as required to evaluate or perform an engagement you request.

Retention

Institutional enquiry records are retained for the duration of the business relationship and as required for legal, audit, and procurement purposes.
