Lifecycle architecture

Phases, counterparties & contract surfaces

Who engages at each stage of the WDDAM lifecycle — and what institutional relationships look like in practice.

Develop Deploy Acquire Manage
01

Develop

Who engages

Enterprise workforce budgets; government programmes; sponsors; co-investors

Delivered

Designed cohorts, Level 1–5 pathways, vendor-aligned credentials

Contract surface

Institutional development & commissioning agreements

e.g. national digital skills mandate, enterprise academy
02

Deploy

Who engages

Operators, primes, system integrators, agencies running live programmes

Delivered

Deployment-ready teams in sector contexts

Contract surface

MSAs, SOWs, programme placement, sector deployment schedules

e.g. hospital systems, energy operators, GovTech rollouts
03

Acquire

Who engages

Corporate HR / procurement; public-sector workforce units; large NGOs

Delivered

Acquisition of verified pipelines and cohort segments

Contract surface

Preferred hire rights, cohort acquisition, contracted talent transfer

e.g. bulk acquisition by level & sector
04

Manage

Who engages

Enterprise & government as ongoing operators of the relationship

Delivered

Performance, upskilling, compliance evidence, capacity optimization

Contract surface

Managed workforce performance, governance reporting, lifecycle systems

e.g. Sector Workforce Director oversight
