WDDAM delivery model

WDDAM — Workforce Development, Deployment, Acquisition & Management — is delivered through the OEDP Work Network with Sector Workforce Director governance, CRM-tracked outcomes, and Level 1–5 cohort units (50 professionals per enterprise cohort).

Certification & exam delivery

  • Pearson VUE — Authorized exam delivery partner (Site ID 89828)
  • CompTIA — Partner; workforce pathways for A+, Network+, Security+, Cloud+
  • EC-Council — Partner; cyber workforce credential infrastructure
  • Microsoft, AWS, Cisco, ASE — Curriculum alignment integrated into sector cohort systems
  • ISO 17024 — Personnel certification framework for institutional reporting (scope documented per engagement)

Certification bodies engage BTH as infrastructure — not as a competing credential publisher. BTH provides cohort systems, exam logistics, institutional sponsor channels, and governed outcomes.
